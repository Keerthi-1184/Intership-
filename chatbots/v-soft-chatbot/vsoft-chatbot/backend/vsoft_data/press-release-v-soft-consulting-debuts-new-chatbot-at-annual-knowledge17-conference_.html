Press Release: V-Soft snowBOT Makes Impression at Knowledge17

Posted on June 28, 2017 (September 3, 2024) by hatfiled developer

June 28, 2017

ORLANDO, Fla. – V-Soft Consulting showed off its new ServiceNow bot, snowBOT, at the Knowledge17 conference in Orlando, Fl in May.

The consulting company was a silver sponsor at ServiceNow’s annual Knowledge conference. While at Knowledge17, V-Soft debuted their new tool, snowBOT. snowBOT is an interactive chatbot that works with the ServiceNow platform and is capable of integrating with a company’s existing messaging systems (such as Skype or Slack,) or directly into the company’s ServiceNow instance. The bot had an excellent reception at the conference, according to V-Soft organizers.

“We were blown away at the response snowBOT received while at the Knowledge17 conference in Orlando, FL. The demand for a user-friendly chatbot capable of automating basic tasks was huge. We are very excited to see where snowBOT takes V-Soft in the future,” said Marketing Coordinator Jessica Gassler.

Along with announcing snowBOT, V-Soft’s Knowledge team attended workshops and lectures to learn more about ServiceNow and industry trends.
