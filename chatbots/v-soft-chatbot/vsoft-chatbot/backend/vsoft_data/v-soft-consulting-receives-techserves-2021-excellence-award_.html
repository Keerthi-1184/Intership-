V-Soft Consulting Receives TechServe’s 2021 Excellence Award

Posted on December 8, 2021 (June 9, 2024) by Michael

V-Soft is one of 17 IT Staffing and Solutions firms recognized.

November 18, 2021

LOUISVILLE, KY

TechServe Alliance presented V-Soft Consulting the 2021 Excellence Award for demonstrating excellence and commitment in IT staffing. TechServe Alliance is the national trade association of the IT & Engineering staffing and solutions industry.

V-Soft has been recognized in the past for its efforts in the IT staffing industry, highlighting V-Soft’s dedication to providing the highest level of service to clients and placing the best talent out there. With the growing V-Soft Digital division on the rise, this award also emphasizes the company’s continued growth in the advanced technology solutions space. 

“This award is a proud accomplishment for us,” said President, Purna Veer. “As an organization, we have worked towards our goals relentlessly. Acknowledgments like this encourage us to continue doing our very best for our clients and consultants. I have to thank TechServe for recognizing us this year.”

This is the second time V-Soft has won the Excellence Award. The first time was in 2019.

“TechServe Alliance is thrilled to honor our 2021 class of Excellence Awards winners. These companies are among the very best in our industry,” says Mark Roberts, CEO of TechServe Alliance.

“For the past six years, I have been impressed by the extraordinary performance demonstrated by our Excellence Awards winners,” he added.

The recipients were honored on November 18th at TechServe’s Executive Summit at the Ritz-Carlton in Amelia Island, Florida. The summit was attended by award recipients and other delegates from the IT staffing industry.

About V-Soft Consulting

V-Soft Consulting Group is headquartered in Louisville, KY with strategic locations across the US and in India. Known as an agile innovative technology services company V-Soft was recently recognized among the top 100 fastest growing staffing companies in North America. V-Soft is a trusted partner with experience across diverse technology stacks to help businesses get IT done. V-Soft has a large quantity of expertise in-house complimented by on-demand talent via their IT staffing division. These compelling facts for business when combined with numerous best-of employer awards makes V-Soft Consulting the employer of choice and the partner of choice for the enterprise.
