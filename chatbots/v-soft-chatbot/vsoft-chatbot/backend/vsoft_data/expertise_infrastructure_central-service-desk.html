24/7 SUPPORT

You can trust V-Soft Call Centers to be there when you need it. With options to have 24/7 support, you can make sure your business stays fully operational even after business hours.

At V-Soft Infrastructure, we understand the importance of efficient and centralized solutions for your company’s diverse needs. Our Central Service Desk provides comprehensive support in various areas, ensuring that your business runs smoothly and effectively.

Single Source Reporting

Our Central Service Desk is committed to offering a single source of reporting for all facets of your company, from IMAC contracts and engagements to invoicing and tracking SLA (Service Level Agreement) statuses. By consolidating all relevant information into one centralized location, you can enjoy the benefits of real-time reporting for your clients, staff, and subcontractors. Say goodbye to scattered data and hello to a more streamlined, organized, and productive operation.

Workflow Management

V-Soft Infrastructure excels at designing tailored workflows to efficiently manage incoming calls and messages, optimizing the handling of issue requests and resolutions. Our continuous reporting system enables us to closely monitor workflows until all problem areas are identified and resolved. Our services encompass proven processes, including call escalation and contact tree management, adapting to the unique requirements of your operation. With our workflow management expertise, you can expect enhanced productivity and seamless issue resolution.
