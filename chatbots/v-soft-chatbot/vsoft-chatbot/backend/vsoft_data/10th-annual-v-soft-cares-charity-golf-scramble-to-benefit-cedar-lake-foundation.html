V-Soft Cares Charity Golf Scramble is teeing off September 30th at Lake Forest Country Club

Posted on July 3, 2024 (September 11, 2024) by Admin

July 2nd, 2024
LOUISVILLE, KY

V-Soft Cares, the philanthropic arm of V-Soft Consulting, is excited to announce a new partnership for the 2024 Golf Scramble. This year we are honored to support Cedar Lake Foundation! Cedar Lake provides residential and community-based care for people with intellectual and developmental disabilities to experience a life of abundant possibilities.

“We are delighted to partner with Cedar Lake Foundation for our 10th annual golf scramble,” said V-Soft President, Purna Veer.” Cedar Lake’s dedication to enriching the lives of individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities resonates deeply with our values. Their unwavering commitment to providing compassionate care and creating opportunities for these individuals inspires us. Supporting Cedar Lake allows us to contribute to a cause that makes a meaningful impact in our community. We look forward to a fantastic event that combines our love for golf with the opportunity to make a difference in the lives of those who need it most.”

Established in 1970, Cedar Lake Foundation has been working towards a community where those with and without disabilities learn to share and enrich each other’s lives through meaningful interactions that cultivate mutual respect and unconditional love.

“At Cedar Lake, we are honored to be the featured charity for the V-Soft Cares Golf Scramble,” said Kathey Sanders, Cedar Lake Foundation’s Executive Director. “The support from V-Soft Cares is invaluable in helping us continue our commitment to providing exceptional care and creating abundant possibilities for those with intellectual and developmental disabilities.”

Marking its tenth year, the V-Soft Cares Annual Charity Golf Scramble is a testament to our commitment to making a difference. Since its inception in 2014, this event has raised over $350,000 in support of local charities, thanks to the generosity and dedication of our partners and supporters.

Join us at the prestigious Lake Forest Country Club to help us support Cedar Lake Foundation by becoming a sponsor, player, or contributor! We welcome monetary donations, swag items, and silent auction treasures. Visit www.vsoftcares.org for more details on how you can get involved and make a lasting impact in our community.
