V-Soft Partners with Industry Leaders to Build the Metaverse

Posted on May 27, 2022 (January 24, 2025) by manoj rauta

V-Soft Digital, its clients, and partners, are paving the way for the workforce of the future

April 18, 2022

LOUISVILLE, KY

The innovation division of V-Soft Consulting, V-Soft Digital, has successfully partnered with thought leaders of both partners and clients, including TechnipFMC and SoftwareAG, to facilitate an industry-leading solution for manufacturing, and engineering firms alike.

Leveraging the power of holographic pane-of-views with augmented reality and AI computer vision, the gap between the organization and practical application of advanced technology has been bridged.

Adam Berg, Manager of Learning Solutions at TechnipFMC, has been leading TechnipFMC’s augmented, immersive, and virtual reality program known as X-Reality or the XR program.

“The purpose of the XR Network is to bring together the knowledge, tools, and people necessary to leverage XR technologies, to achieve business goals, and transform industry environments,” added Adam Berg.

V-Soft Digital extends XR technologies with artificial intelligence capabilities that enable critical steps to successfully deploy them into production use-cases. Building these industrial metaverse gateways provide meaningful value for digital workers.

“Augmented reality implementations have unique requirements for them to be scalable across the workforce,” said Konrad Konarski, VP of AI, IoT and AR at V-Soft Digital. “What we are doing with our clients and partners is empowering manufacturers and oil and gas organizations with the necessary tools to make AR and industrial metaverse a real part of their digital evolution.”

Technologies like artificial intelligence in the form of Natural Language Processing (NLP), for example, serve as accelerated pathways to take data structured for consumption in the real world and rebuild it for relevant context in augmented reality.  In addition, technologies such as digital twins and AI are apart of the essential building blocks to construct the industrial metaverse.

SoftwareAG, an enterprise software and technology company with over 10,000 customers, has been working closely with V-Soft Digital to expand this collective effort.

Using their industry-leading IoT platform, Cumulocity IoT, the two organizations extend the ability of traditional IoT device management with future-of-work centric hardware systems that includes edge-AI systems and augmented reality wearables.

“We see IoT and analytics playing a critical role in building, scaling, and management of XR deployments,” said Ricky Singh Vice-President of IoT Americas at SoftwareAG. “Our Cumulocity platform was built with the diversity of these use cases in mind – it can be deployed across a variety of different hardware types, spanning the edge-to-cloud continuum to bring new use cases to life.”

At MxD, the leading cybersecurity and digital manufacturing center in the U.S., the results of this partnership can be seen in action. Augmented reality, artificial intelligence, robotics, and IoT technologies come together to provide a real-world view of the industrial metaverse.
